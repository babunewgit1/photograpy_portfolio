"Promise" is a visual exploration of a frozen universe, an immersion into winter landscapes where cold colors and ski outfits dominate. this photo shoot project captures the very essence of cold, transforming each image into a unique sensory experience.

the photos of "Promise" are enveloped in shades of blue and white, evoking the purity and chill of polar climates. the subtle nuances of blue create a serene and mysterious ambiance, accentuating the sensation of intense cold.

the protagonists of this project are dressed in sophisticated and functional ski outfits. The white and black suits contrast with the backgrounds, highlighting both the technicality and elegance of the clothing. the helmets and ski masks add a futuristic touch.

"Promise" is more than just a series of photos; it is an invitation to feel the cold, to plunge into an environment where every breath of air seems to crystallize. the textures of the clothing and accessories blend with the icy background.
